Okiya is a game in which two okiyas (geisha houses) compete for the favour of the Empiror.
The competition takes place in the imperial garden (game field) in which there are 4 types of plants:lilys, maples, pines and sakuras.
In addition to plants there are other elements: sun, rain, birds and tanzakus.
The first player starts by picking a tile from the game field. The next player can only pick a tile that contains a plant or an element from the previous tile.
How to win?
- The player who first occupies a horizontal, vertial or a diagonal row of 4 tiles wins.
- The player who first occupies a 2*2 square of 4 tiles wins.
- A player who leaves no turns for the opponent wins.
